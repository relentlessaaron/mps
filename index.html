WHY MPS?

It's important that your production has veterans of the trade involved for numerous reasons. MPS offers numerous trucks with a wide array of options. We have all the industry standards from Chimera, Arri, Kino Flo and Mole Richardson as well as the hard to find lighting and grip items. Our HMI compliment ranges from 200W to 18,000W, and are ready for even your most demanding work. We've got the effects equipment, portable generators, and multiple dollies round out our capability. In addition to these services, MPS. has worked with many production studios and sets.
Please allow us to fill your lighting needs. We aim to please.
